  • Larchmont Hotel
    ($110+ / night : affordable & convenient)
    • $

    Call 212-989-9333 or book online at www.larchmonthotel.com. You can cancel up until 48 hours before checkin with no penalty.

    A "European style" hotel that is just 3 blocks from the reception venue. The rooms are clean and modest with shared hallway bathrooms. For the price, proximity to the reception and quiet block, this is the most affordable choice for a nearby hotel.

  • Hyatt Union Square
    ($275 / night : luxury & convenience)

    Call 212-253-1234 or book online at www.unionsquare.hyatt.com.

    A luxury hotel off Union Square that is a quick walk to the reception. This hotel has a nice bar, restaurant and gym on premmises. The hotel is only one year old with very great rooms. The groom's family and a few guests have confirmed here.

  • These are a few places we have found that are near the wedding venues and are reasonably priced. However the best rates can be found on hotels.com or kayak.com or a similar discount booking site.   • The Kitano
    ($200 / night : mid-range luxury)

    Call 212-885-7000 / 1-800-548-2666 or email reservations@kitano.com and mention the Christensen & Counihan wedding block. Wedding block price expires June 25 and you can cancel up until 72 hours before checkin with no penalty.

    A nice hotel with large rooms that's about halfway between the church and the reception venue with about a $10 taxi to either one. The subway at Grand Central Station is a 7 minute walk from this hotel. This hotel is great for those looking for larger rooms than the Hyatt at a more affordable price and don't mind a quick taxi ride.
