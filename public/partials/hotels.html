Larchmont Hotel
$110+ / night : affordable & convenient :

Call 212-989-9333 or book online at www.larchmonthotel.com. You can cancel up until 48 hours before checkin with no penalty.

A "European style" hotel that is just 3 blocks from the reception venue. The rooms are clean and modest with shared hallway bathrooms. For the price, proximity to the reception and quiet block, this is the most affordable choice for a nearby hotel.

Hyatt Union Square
$279 / night : luxury & convenience :

We are under the "Christensen/Counihan Wedding Block" for a Courtyard Studio at $279/night. For reservations call the central reservations system at 1-888-591-1234, or book directly from wwww.hyattunionsquarenyc.com using the Group # G-CHCO.

A luxury hotel off Union Square that is a quick walk to the reception. This hotel has a nice bar, restaurant and gym on premises. The hotel is only one year old with new, modest-sized rooms. The groom's family and a few guests have confirmed here.

The Kitano
$200 / night : mid-range luxury :

Call 212-885-7000 / 1-800-548-2666 or email reservations@kitano.com and mention the Christensen & Counihan wedding block. Wedding block price expires June 25 and you can cancel up until 72 hours before checkin with no penalty.

A nice hotel with large rooms that's halfway between the church and the reception venue with roughly a $15 taxi to either one. The subway at Grand Central Station is a 7 minute walk from this hotel. This hotel is great for those looking for larger rooms than the Hyatt at a more affordable price and don't mind a quick taxi ride.
